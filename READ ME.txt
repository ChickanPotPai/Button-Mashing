Welcome to Button Mashing!

The objective of this game is to kill all mobs before
they hit you. Upon touching a mob, you will take damage depending
on the size of the mob. Kill everything before getting killed.

Controls:
A - Move Left
D - Move Right
Q - Attack(0 Charge)
E - Fire Projectile(40 Charge)
R - Fire Special(100 Charge)

` - Close game(Required to close game properly)
M - Return to settings


Attacking will grant charge per hit, specials 
will heal a small amount of health.

----------------------------------------------------------------


In this program I used classes as an easier way to
create and animate the objects.

I assigned variables and functions to each object,
calling each one as needed, making it easier to work
with animating each object.
